20/04/25
i was working a few days at the farm of jörg lauenroth-mago in April 2025. it is a farm in sachsen-anhalt near magdeburg with horses, cows, chickens and a garden, i wanted to work at. a field had very dry, sandy grounds. greißkraut, a yellow flower was easy to find. it is not eatable for cows because it has a poison. in hay it becomes dangerous because the poison remain preserved. so we tried to find every flower over the whole meadow ground. on another day, we had to control meadows on existing flowers/identification herbs, which are necessary to the demeter-certification. with the app flora incognita we walked over the whole ground, another acre than before, to identify mainly these herbs: knöllchen-steinprech, scharfer hahnenfuß, gamander ehrenpreis, wiesen-margharithe, johanniskraut - tüpfel harteu und labkraut.

it was a first approach to have a job in gardening. dependant upon my experiences it became a bad situation. my talent were the flowers but they took someone male and experienced in wood-work and machinery.
